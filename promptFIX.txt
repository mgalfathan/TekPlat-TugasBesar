Provider priority:
1. API-Football for complete data and live/realtime features.
2. football-data.org as fallback/basic provider.
3. seed data as offline fallback.

Why:
API-Football supports broader football data such as livescore, fixtures, standings, teams, player statistics, events, lineups, odds, and predictions.
football-data.org is useful for basic competition, teams, matches, and standings, but should not be the only provider.
